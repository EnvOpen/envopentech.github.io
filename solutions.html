Adoption Approaches For Different Contexts

Stellarae projects are designed to fit multiple deployment patterns. Whether you're adding to existing systems or building something new, start where it makes sense for your team.

Proof of Concept

Evaluate in local development. Test the architecture, APIs, and operational model.

Staged Rollout

Deploy to staging first with full security posture. Validate audit trails and operational procedures before production.

Production Deployment

Run with high availability, strict key management, monitoring, and incident runbooks. Full transparency and auditability.

Getting Started

  1. Review project documentation and architecture.
  2. Run locally or in containerized environment for testing.
  3. Read and understand the code—no black boxes.
  4. Customize as needed for your environment.
  5. Deploy with your operational standards and monitoring.
Each Stellarae project is independent and self-contained. Use one or combine multiple. You're never locked into a specific deployment or operational model.
