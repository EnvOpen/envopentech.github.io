Our Mission

Env Open is an open-source first software foundation dedicated to creating and maintaining projects that make a positive impact on the world. We believe that open source software is the foundation of innovation and collaboration in the digital age.

Our team consists of passionate developers who are committed to building tools and platforms that empower others to create, learn, and succeed. Every project we develop follows our core principle: Open Source, Forever.
