Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to your projects?

There are many ways to contribute! You can submit code via pull requests, report bugs, improve documentation, or help with testing. Check out our GitHub repositories for specific contribution guidelines for each project.

Do you accept project proposals?

Absolutely! We're always interested in new project ideas that align with our mission. Send us an email with your proposal, including the problem you're solving and how it benefits the open-source community.

Can I use your projects in commercial applications?

Yes! All our projects are open-source and free to use in both personal and commercial applications. Check the specific license for each project for detailed terms and conditions.

How do you ensure project sustainability?

We're committed to maintaining our projects long-term. We have clear governance structures, documentation standards, and welcome community maintainers to ensure projects remain healthy and sustainable.

Do you offer mentorship for new contributors?

Yes! We believe in helping developers grow. We provide guidance for first-time contributors and offer mentorship opportunities for those looking to develop their open-source skills.

How do you decide which projects to work on?

We prioritize projects that solve real problems, benefit the developer community, and align with our values of openness and collaboration. Community feedback and needs assessment play a key role in our decision-making.
